# Launch Kit — Weekly Finance Brief

Ready-to-post copy + a realistic plan. Product is LIVE at **https://weeklyfinancebrief.com**.
Honest framing throughout (just launched, built solo) — for HN/IH that converts better than hype, and it's true.

---

## The one-liner (memorise this)

> One plain-English email a week on what happened in markets, why it matters, and what to watch next — for busy people with investments, not finance pros. 5-minute read, free for a week.

**Positioning:** not "alpha" or stock tips. It's *orientation* — the calm weekly recap so you're not reading financial media all day. Educational, source-traced, never advice.

---

## Reality check (read first)

- Your first ~10 subscribers come from **people who already trust you**, not cold posts. DM friends/colleagues/family who invest. This beats every channel below combined at the start.
- Cold channels (HN, Reddit, PH) give a **spike**, not a flywheel. Expect a handful of signups per launch, learn from feedback, iterate.
- The **engineering story** ("built solo, writes & sends itself for ~$0/month, every figure source-traced") is more upvote-able on HN/IH than the product itself. Lead with it there.
- Be ready: when you post, the OG card renders nicely and the free trial is one click. The funnel is ready.

## Don't-forget polish before a big push

- Stripe public name still says "…sandbox" → Settings → Business → public details (receipts/checkout show this).
- Have 2–3 real issues in the archive so visitors see substance (the pipeline adds one/week automatically).
- Consider a free preview/sample issue link prominent on the homepage so people can read before subscribing (the `/issues` archive already does this).
